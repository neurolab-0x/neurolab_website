# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct (v2.0)

## Our Pledge
We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in our project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Accepting constructive criticism gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Unacceptable behavior includes (but is not limited to):
- Harassment, discrimination, or hateful language
- Personal attacks or insults
- Public or private harassment

## Enforcement
Report incidents to the project maintainers. Reports will be reviewed and acted upon at the maintainers' discretion.
